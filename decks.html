Decks by NOVA Build Pros

NOVA Build Pros custom designs and builds the highest quality wood and composite decks for all of Northern Virginia. And we've been doing it for over 20 years!. Our custom designs fit any budget and any situation whether it is ground-level or over three stories.

As a deck contractor all of our decks meet or exceed International Building Code and all codes and standards for your locality. We offer wood and composite decking in all color combinations to suit your need, or to satisy your Home Owner's Association regulations. We take the stress off you by managing all the design and permitting paperwork. And we'll even provide copies of designs and licenses that may be required by your HOA.

We build composite decks using deck material from:
•Trex
•AZEK
•Cali-Bamboo
